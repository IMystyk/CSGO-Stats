To download data from steam press 'Get data' button. 
You will be redirected to steam page, where you will have 60 seconds to log in. 
After that, you will have another 10 seconds to choose either competitive or wingman matches. 
There is no indication how long loading all the matches might take. 
Afther the page has loaded a console log will open, informing you about progress. 
The results are then stored in 'results.txt' file, and notification appears on this screen.
